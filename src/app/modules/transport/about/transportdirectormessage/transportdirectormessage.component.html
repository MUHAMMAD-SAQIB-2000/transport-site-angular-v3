Managing Director Message

A key purpose of transport systems is to provide people with access to destinations. A growing body of academic literature has delivered extensive evidence of the, sometimes large, disparities in accessibility between population groups.

At UOG, we are trying our best to provide students with best Buses and experienced drivers so they don't face any difficulty in reaching university boundaries, for the want of education.
